our food philosophy...

As parents, we believe in feeding our children with only the best and healthiest foods and ingredients. We wouldn’t choose anything less for your children! Everything from our kitchen is made to order, and fresh daily. We use natural, organic, local, and sustainable options wherever possible. Our meats are all natural and preservative free. Our produce is local and fresh. We have gluten free and vegetarian options. Come in and try out our delicious foods and snacks! We want your children to have all the energy to play and enjoy their time here, but that doesn’t have to mean fast food and candy.

remember, you are what you eat!
